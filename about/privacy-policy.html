Privacy Policy for vebscraper

Welcome to vebscraper

vebscraper is a free online web scraping tool designed to provide users with valuable insights into various aspects of web pages. This Privacy Policy outlines how vebscraper collects, uses, and safeguards your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect:

a. User-provided Information:

vebscraper does not require any user registration or login. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.

b. Web Scraping Data:

When you use vebscraper to extract information from web pages, the tool processes and displays the requested data, including but not limited to titles, meta descriptions, headings, images, links, canonical tags, Open Graph tags, page data, social media links, HTTPS status, common words, and security measurements. vebscraper does not store the data you input or extract during the web scraping process.

How We Use Your Information:

a. Web Scraping Data:

The information extracted using vebscraper is displayed in real-time for immediate use. vebscraper does not retain or store the data you input or extract during the web scraping process.

Data Security:

a. No Storage:

vebscraper does not store any data provided by users or the data extracted during the web scraping process. All information is processed and displayed in real-time, and there is no retention of user data.

Security Measurements:

a. Anonymous Usage:

vebscraper performs security measurements without requiring users to log in or provide any personal information. These measurements are conducted for the purpose of enhancing the security of our platform.

Cookies:

a. No Cookies:

vebscraper does not use cookies for tracking or any other purposes.

Third-Party Links:

a. External Links:

vebscraper may provide external links to third-party websites for reference purposes.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

a. Notification of Changes:

vebscraper may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes by the updated date at the beginning of this document.

Contact Us:

a. Questions and Concerns:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or vebscraper's privacy practices, please contact us at vebscraper@gmail.com.

By using vebscraper, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. It is recommended to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

Thank you for choosing vebscraper!
